We offer a quick and easy solution to upcycle your clothes Along with the Assurance that it's going to the right place i.e not being dumped in a landfill, burnt, We do that with our unique process Wherein we collect clothes, easing the process of humans who are trying to do the right thing, sorting them into three levels of usability, transporting them to certified organizations who upcycle clothes and restore them, ensuring that Share our values, and vision on tackling this issue The organizations we handpicked them through extensive research

LEVEL O

What we do?

This level consists of all those fabrics and clothes that have been worn very less and are in a condition to be sold .

LEVEL 1

This level consists of all those fabrics and clothes that are not in a state to be sold again but the fabric can be upcycled. These fabrics are sorted and sent off to centers where they can be revamped into newer, cooler things

LEVEL 2

This level consists of everything beyond the point of repair, fabric that can not be used for upcycling, nearing the waste level. These fabrics are used for many other uses that don't necessarily involve making new clothes
